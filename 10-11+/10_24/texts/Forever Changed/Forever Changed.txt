Forever Changed was a Christian Rock band from Tallahassee and Orlando, Florida.  They came together in 1999 and broke up in 2006.[2]  Dan Cole was the lead singer, a guitarist, and a pianist.  Ben O'Rear was the lead guitarist, Tom Gustafson played bass, and Nathan Lee played the drums.They released three albums: Drifting into Amazing in 2002, The Need to Feel Alive in 2005[3] and Chapters in 2006.[4]
They were the first band in Tallahassee to obtain a sponsorship by Chipotle.
