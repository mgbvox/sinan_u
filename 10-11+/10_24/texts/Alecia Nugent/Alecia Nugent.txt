
Alecia Nugent (born in Hickory Grove, Louisiana) is a country, Americana, bluegrass vocalist and musician.  Alecia has worked with bluegrass legend Carl Jackson on all of her albums.  Alecia was nominated for IBMA Female Vocalist of The Year in 2009.[1][2]
Nugent performed in Scarborough, Ontario, Canada in 2008 as part of the Bluegrass Sundays Winter Concert Series.[3]
