Josh Shilling (born in Martinsville, Virginia) is an American musician.  He joined the bluegrass band Mountain Heart in 2007.[1]  Shilling's debut with Mountain Heart was at The Grand Ole Opry in January 2007, where he received a standing ovation. Josh's impact on the band has been noted in many interviews including 
one in "District Noise".[2]
In 2012, Shilling released his first solo album, "Letting Go",[3] and his first Christmas solo, "The Christmas Song".[4]
Shilling co-hosts a show, "That Just Happened", on historical WSM Radio along with band mates Jim VanCleve and Seth Taylor.
